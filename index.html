Our School

Akshara Public School is a CBSE school that was established in May 2019, through the Shri Mahalakshmi Charitable Trust promoted by its managing trustees. The school is situated at Mahalakshmi Nagar on the outskirts of Vedasanur, on a sprawling campus of 4 acres and provides peaceful learning environment.
